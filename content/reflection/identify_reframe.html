Identify and Reframe

Identifying and reframing the challenge required iterative discussions and reflection, particularly given my engineering background. Initially, I focused on finding solutions, but this approach did not align with the project’s goal of analyzing systemic issues.

Collaboration
Collaborating with my team, I learned to consider perspectives beyond technical fixes, such as cultural and ecological factors. Consulting local experts like Dr. Jo Leen clarified the broader context of biodiversity and urbanisation, leading to a reframed challenge highlighting the government’s failure to prioritize green space preservation.
This process taught me to integrate interdisciplinary insights, improving my ability to approach challenges beyond engineering constraints.
